How to Make a Crispy Omelet

A step-by-step guide to achieve the perfect omelet, crispy, fluffy, and flavorful.

  • Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of water or milk (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

  • Equipment

  • Mixing bowl
  • Fork or whisk
  • Nonstick skillet
  • Spatula

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a mixing bowl. Add water or milk (optional) and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Whisk the eggs vigorously until well combined and frothy.
  3. Heat oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Test the oil with a drop of egg mixture; if it sizzles, it's ready.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the hot oil. Gently push cooked edges towards the center as the eggs cook.
  5. Once the bottom is set and edges are brown, carefully flip the omelet using a spatula.
  6. Cook for another minute or two, or until desired doneness. Slide onto a plate and serve immediately.

Additional Tips

* Add a teaspoon of cornstarch or rice flour for extra crispiness. * Add fillings like cheese, vegetables, or meat before flipping. * Experiment with flavors by adding herbs, spices, or hot sauce. * Practice makes perfect!
